ChatRoleWeaver Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

ChatRoleWeaver is an independent browser extension that provides roleplay and story-management tools for supported AI chat websites.

Information ChatRoleWeaver stores

ChatRoleWeaver may store information that you enter into the extension, such as character details, scene information, journal entries, inventory items, layout preferences, theme settings, and other session data.

This information is stored locally in your browser using browser storage.

Information we collect

ChatRoleWeaver does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share your personal information, chat conversations, browsing history, roleplay content, uploaded images, or locally stored extension data with the developer or third parties.

Website access

ChatRoleWeaver requests access to supported AI chat websites so it can display its sidebar, insert user-approved context, and provide its roleplay tools on those pages.

This access is used only to provide the extension’s user-facing functionality.

Uploaded images

Images selected for character portraits or scene backgrounds are processed and stored locally in your browser unless otherwise clearly stated by the extension.

Third-party services

ChatRoleWeaver operates alongside third-party AI chat services. Your use of those services is governed by their own terms and privacy policies. ChatRoleWeaver is not affiliated with or endorsed by those services.

Data retention and deletion

Locally stored ChatRoleWeaver data remains in your browser until you remove it through the extension, clear browser or extension storage, or uninstall the extension.

Limited Use

ChatRoleWeaver’s use of information received from supported websites is limited to providing and improving the extension’s clearly disclosed user-facing features. ChatRoleWeaver does not use this information for advertising, credit decisions, or sale to third parties.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated if ChatRoleWeaver’s features or data practices change. The latest version will always be published on this page.
